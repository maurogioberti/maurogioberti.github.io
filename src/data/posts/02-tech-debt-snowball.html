Tech debt might seem benign at first, like a small snowball. But leave it unchecked, and it can snowball into an avalanche, hampering innovation and slowing down progress.

Why avoid it?

It complicates future development and can degrade your system's integrity.

Tackling it

Regularly review and address tech debt before it accumulates. Small refactoring efforts can prevent larger issues.

Prevention

Invest in good coding practices from the start and allocate time for addressing potential tech debt in your development cycle.

Let's not let tech debt snowball into an obstacle. Keep it manageable and your projects innovative and efficient!
