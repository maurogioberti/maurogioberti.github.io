🚨 Don’t expect feedback unless you ask: Don’t assume silence means all is good. No one will tell you anything unless you actively ask for feedback.

⚠️ Overconfidence trap: As time passes, you may start thinking everything is going well, but it might not be as you think.

🌱 Professional growth: Even if feedback is negative, it's a key opportunity to improve and grow in your career.

🏆 Celebrate unnoticed wins: You may have done something really good and not realized it, allowing you to be recognized for your efforts.

🤝 Strengthen relationships: Requesting feedback shows humility and a genuine attitude, builds trust and opens communication channels.

🎙️ So, don’t mute yourself 🎤: Speak up, 📢 raise your voice, and be heard! 🔥💪
